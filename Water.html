Methods For Water Conservation

  • Check for leaks in pipes, hoses faucets and couplings

  • Use a broom to clean driveways, sidewalks and steps

  • Install water-saving shower heads or flow restrictors

  • Turn off the water while brushing your teeth

  • Use your automatic dishwasher for full loads only

  • Use your automatic washing machine only for full loads only

  • If you wash dishes by hand, don't leave the water running for rinsing

  • Rain water harvesting is the best and most suitable method

  • Use an irrigation system which uses water efficiently and prevent wasting

  • Close Taps Properly and Fix Leaking Taps, Pipes & Toilets
